Auditorium Booking Information

This website is used for booking the auditorium. Any institute within the campus is welcome to submit a booking request.

Booking Guidelines

  • Prior Approval: Please obtain approval from your respective HOD or Principal before proceeding with the booking.
  • Booking Confirmation: Once you have received the necessary approval, book the slot on this website. Then:
    • Download the booking receipt.
    • Get a permission letter signed by your Principal or HOD.
    • Submit both the receipt and the permission letter to the Admin Office.
  • Final Approval: The booking will be confirmed only after the Admin verifies and approves the submitted documents.
