Auditorium Booking Information

This website serves as a convenient platform for booking our college auditorium. All institutes within the campus are welcome to submit a booking request for their events and activities.

Booking Guidelines

  1. Prior Approval: Please ensure you obtain necessary approval from your respective Head of Department (HOD) or Principal before initiating any booking request on this website. This step is crucial for smooth processing.
  2. Booking Confirmation & Document Submission: Once you have secured the required internal approval and submitted your booking request online:
    • Download the **booking receipt** generated by the system.
    • Obtain a **permission letter** specifically signed by your Principal or HOD for the event.
    • Submit both the downloaded receipt and the signed permission letter to the **Admin Office** for verification.
  3. Final Approval: Your auditorium booking will be considered fully confirmed only after the Admin Office has successfully verified and officially approved the submitted documents. You will receive a notification upon final approval.
